Beginner's self-defense Course

This course is designed for individuals who are new to self-defense training. The focus of this course is on developing basic skills and techniques that will help you defend yourself in common real-life situations.

The course will cover various aspects of self-defense, including basic striking, blocking, and grappling techniques. You will also learn about situational awareness and how to avoid dangerous situations altogether.

The course is taught by experienced instructors who have years of experience in self-defense and martial arts training. They will provide you with personalized instruction and feedback to help you improve your skills.
