Welcome to the DOBU Martial Art

DoBu Martial Arts is a martial arts gym. The gym offers classes in a number of different martial arts, as well as fitness training and self-defence courses. The gym has a large, matted martial arts area, a fully-equipped gym, a sauna, a steam room and changing and shower facilities

Gym and facilities of Dobu Martial Art


Gym and Fitness room of Dobu Martial art

Welcome to Dobu Martial Art! Our gym is a top-of-the-line facility that offers everything you need to achieve your fitness and martial arts goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced martial artist, we have something for everyone. In addition to our world-class facilities, we offer a variety of classes and programs to suit your needs and interests. Our classes range from beginner-level to advanced, and we offer everything from traditional martial arts like karate and taekwondo to more modern styles like MMA and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. We also offer specialized programs like self-defense classes and fitness-focused workouts. Moreover, you can choose the instructor to have the private tuition with you too. "Try it Yourself"
